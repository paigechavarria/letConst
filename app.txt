Quiz

- What is the difference between var and let?

the differnece between var and let is that var can be redeclared and reassigned
but let can only be reassigned and not redeclared

- What is the difference between var and const?

the difference between var and const is that const can neither be redeclared and reassigned.

- What is the difference between let and const?

the difference between let and const is that let can be reassigned and const cannot. 

- What is hoisting?

hoisting is the act of 'hoisting' the variables to the top of the code
so that you may access them before they are actually being used.
